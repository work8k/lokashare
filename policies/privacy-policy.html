Privacy Policy

Stored Locally

The address you add in Loka Share is saved locally on your device. We don’t have access to what you type or save.

Backup

You can back up your data using Apple's iCloud Backup. This is managed entirely by Apple and follows Apple's privacy policies.

How Sharing Works

When you enter an address or location in Loka Share and tap Share, iOS opens the system Share Sheet. You then choose the destination app (for example, WhatsApp, Messages, Mail, or others).

  • The content you choose to share is copied to the app you selected, under your direction.
  • Loka Share does not transmit or store this content on our servers. The sending happens inside the chosen app and is governed by that app's privacy policy.
  • You decide what to share and with whom.

Basic App Usage Stats

To improve Loka Share, and for anonymous analysis purposes only, we collect a small amount of app-usage data that is not linked to you at all.

This data is anonymous and not linked to you.

We use Google Firebase Analytics to measure this basic app usage. We do not collect or send the data you enter.
